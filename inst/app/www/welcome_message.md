## Welcome to the WISE-APP

**Weather Impact Simulation and Evaluation for Adaptation Policy and Planning**

This tool is designed to help you understand the relationship between welfare and weather.

Follow the steps to perform simulations and explore policy scenarios:

1. **Model welfare** — Derive the statistical relationship between weather and outcomes of interest. The fitted model is the foundation for all subsequent steps.
2. **Simulate welfare** — Apply the fitted model to historical and future climate scenarios to produce a distribution of weather-driven outcomes.
3. **Policy scenarios** _<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">[Under development]</span>_ — Re-simulate outcomes under counterfactual policy settings to quantify direct welfare gains and changes in climate resilience.
